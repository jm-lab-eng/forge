Robotic Tank Cleaning System

Problem: Manual cleaning inside hazardous industrial tanks posed safety risks and inefficiencies.

Design Challenges:

  • Confined space operation
  • Corrosive environment
  • Remote accessibility

Engineering Approach:

  • Designed modular robotic structure
  • Optimized material selection for corrosion resistance
  • Integrated remote operation capability

Result:

  • Improved cleaning efficiency by ~35%
  • Reduced human exposure to hazardous conditions
