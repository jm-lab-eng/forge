👤 About Me

I am a Mechanical Design Engineer with over 4 years of experience building real-world, field-deployed systems in robotics and industrial environments. I enjoy taking ideas from concept to deployment—translating complex engineering challenges into practical, reliable, and manufacturable solutions.

My work has involved system-level mechanical design, hydraulic integration, structural optimisation, and validation under actual operating conditions—ensuring safety, durability, and performance where it truly matters. I have worked across multiple robotic and industrial systems, with a strong focus on electro-mechanical integration, material selection, and design for manufacturability.

I’m particularly drawn to solving problems with high constraints—tight spaces, harsh environments, and safety-critical requirements. Beyond design, I have coordinated cross-functional teams and supported engineering activities across design, fabrication, and testing phases, helping drive projects from development to deployment.

I value clarity in design, simplicity in solutions, and systems that perform reliably in the field—not just on paper. My core tools include SolidWorks, AutoCAD, and ANSYS (FEA), which I use to support design, analysis, and validation in demanding applications.
