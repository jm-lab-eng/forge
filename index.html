01 About

Building systems that perform where it counts

I'm a Mechanical Design Engineer with over 4 years of experience building real-world, field-deployed systems in robotics and industrial environments. I take ideas from concept to deployment — translating complex engineering challenges into practical, reliable, and manufacturable solutions.

My work spans system-level mechanical design, hydraulic integration, structural optimisation, and validation under actual operating conditions — ensuring safety, durability, and performance where it truly matters.

I'm particularly drawn to high-constraint problems: tight spaces, harsh environments, and safety-critical requirements. Beyond design, I coordinate cross-functional teams and support engineering activities across design, fabrication, and testing phases.
